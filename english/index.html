ABOUT US

We started in 2019 only with a private network as a customer base and based ourselves for a period on hired labor. We spent about 6 months building up enough customers to get the operation going. This half year we were located in a small office at Økern in Oslo. The company moved in September 2019 to the current premises in Schultz gate 33 at Majorstuen in Oslo.

We want all companies to have clear and correctly updated accounts at all times. The companies' financial situation is the company's most important information for decisions concerning operations, investments, new hires, etc. The customers' financial condition must be clear and easy to interpret, so that owners can make the best decisions on behalf of the company.
